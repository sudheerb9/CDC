Facilities for Recruiters:

  • The Recruiters are provided with all the required facilities for the placement process as mentioned below.
  1. The placement office is fully furnished, air conditioned and equipped with computer and internet facilities.
  2. Any recruiter interested in delivering a Pre-Placement Talk is provided with an auditorium having a capacity of 200 and other required facilities, if any.
  3. Fully furnished, air conditioned rooms for group discussion and personal interviews.
  4. Video conferencing facilities are available if necessary.
